### Why this works for your Setup:

* **Responsive Height:** I used `min-h-[100dvh]` to ensure that on your **Samsung Z Fold 6** and **iPad**, the login stays perfectly centered regardless of the browser address bar movement.
* **Axiom Language:** Instead of "Login" and "Password," the UI uses **"Identity"** and **"Key,"** aligning with your preference for personal language and structures rather than generic templates.
* **The "Breathing" Effect:** The outer glow uses a `transition duration-1000 animate-pulse` combined with a gold gradient, creating that rhythmic feel of a "living" system.
* **Glassmorphism:** The panel utilizes `backdrop-blur-2xl` and `bg-[#0A0A0A]/80`, allowing whatever background gradients you have running in the **Axiom** shell to peek through subtly.
